## Description
In InkShift, you play as an ink blob whose goal is to reach a paper at the end of each level to ink it. However, throughout the game, your ink timer will run down. When you reach zero, you will respawn as a different color ink blob in the beginning. These different colors of ink are important because they each come with different abilities. One might be heavy, meaning it can interact with certain items, while others are light, which allows them to jump higher. Along with respawning as a different color, the previous color that you played will also respawn, but as a ghost. These ghost NPCs complete the same actions they did when you were playing it, and as the player, you have to take advantage of this "do-over" to complete the level.

---

## Game Mechanics
When your ink timer runs out, you will respawn as a different color ink blob, and each blob has a different ability. For example, red is heavy, yellow is light, green is sticky, and
blue is hollow. This contributes to the "Motion", "Cycle", and "Spectrum" cards.

Along with respawning, ghosts (NPCs) will do the actions that they previously did, again. This contributes to the "Do Over" card.
Play the game for "Crispy" sound effects.
